### Steps to Set Up and Run the Application

How to use the christie hotel booking application.

#### To search for a room type

First navigate to the homepage.
Then locate the search bar (if you click it will populate room type options, or you can type in your desired room type)
You may then click on any of the rooms that populate the site to be snet to a small page with information for that room.

#### To Make A Booking

Either on the homepage or a room data page there will be a button saying (Book this room or Make A Booking)
Click that button which will redirect you to a form fill out that form with valid data and click confirming booking, if all data was entered properly  and there are no time conflicts you won't have any errors.

#### To Check A Booking

Navigate to the home page and click check booking, otherwise after you make a booking you will be redirected to the form where you can confirm your booking
Be sure to enter your full name and checkIN date and your booking will appear.
